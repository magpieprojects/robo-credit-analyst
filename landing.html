Robo_Credit_Analyst

// Automating Credit Portfolio Intelligence

In corporate banking and asset management, risk teams spend hours diagnosing why a portfolio's risk profile changed between two reporting dates. Data needs to be extracted, aggregated, and drilled down to individual counterparty levels before a human can write a single sentence of explanation.

The Robo Credit Analyst is a Business Intelligence MVP that acts as an autonomous data analyst, executing a deterministic, 3-step analytical workflow to pinpoint the root cause of risk shifts instantly.

> The_Workflow.sh

# We ask the LLM to write exact SQL against a strict schema.

STEP 1: check_portfolio_drift()
/* "Did the overall risk change?" */
exec SQL: SUM(pd * exposure) / SUM(exposure)
if delta != 0: continue

STEP 2: identify_primary_driver()
/* "Who is mathematically responsible?" */
exec SQL: calculate individual counterparty contributions.
return top_contributing_customer_id

STEP 3: generate_root_cause()
/* "What exactly happened to this driver?" */
exec SQL: fetch historical facts (Rating, PD, Exposure) for top driver.
invoke LLM: "Write a strict 10-word summary explaining the shift based on these facts."

> Why_It_Matters.md

  • Explainability over Black Box: The AI isn't guessing. It writes deterministic SQL to calculate math, then summarizes the factual result.
  • Auditability: Every step of the thought process, including the generated SQL and raw data results, is visible to the user.
  • Speed to Insight: What takes a junior analyst an hour in Excel happens in seconds via an API call.
